About HASAA

Since its inception, the Hindustan Antibiotics School has been the alma-mater for thousands of successful students who have gone on to make a name for themselves in various fields across the world. Many of these alumni continue to share a deep emotional bond with their school and have long wished to contribute meaningfully to its progress and development.

Over the years, several alumni visited the school and expressed their desire to give back. However, in the absence of a formally established alumni association, these efforts remained scattered and limited in impact.

In 2011, a group of alumni from the 1998 SSC batch decided to take a more structured and long-term approach. After extensive groundwork and interactions with school authorities, teachers, administrators, the school committee, the D. E. Society, and fellow alumni, they laid the foundation for a formally registered alumni body.

This effort received immense encouragement and support from Mr. Burse (Headmaster, Secondary Unit), Mrs. Dongre (Headmistress, Primary Unit), Dr. Savita Kelkar (Chairperson, School Committee), along with many other dedicated teachers and staff members.

The registration of the Hindustan Antibiotics School Alumni Association, abbreviated as HASAA, was completed in March 2012 under Registration No. MAH 348/2012/Pune. HASAA is the official alumni association of Hindustan Antibiotics School, formally recognised and endorsed by both the school authorities and the D. E. Society.

HASAA aims to reconnect and engage the extensive alumni network that Hindustan Antibiotics School has built over the decades, and to create a common platform through which alumni can contribute to the school and benefit from mutual collaboration and support.

If you are an alumnus of Hindustan Antibiotics School, we warmly invite you to join HASAA and become a member. You may register online or download the membership form and submit it by post.
